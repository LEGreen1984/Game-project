Interrogation

Suspect:Kiyoko Fujiwara

So... this is Kiyoko the wife of Kenji. She doesn't look at me as she walks towards the chair. When I say her name she flinches like an abused puppy. Her eyes are bloodshot. She's been crying a lot. I couldn't imagine this woman being able to face a spider, let alone murder her husband. Then again... the great thing about poison is you don't need a lot of courage. All you're doing is pouring a bottle into a bowl. No different to adding pepper. Until they drop dead.

Kiyoko...why do you look so on edge? Is it because you're really the damsel in distress... or you think playing that part will hide what you've done?
