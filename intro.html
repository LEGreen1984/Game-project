The Case

The businessman's son

At 20:21pm yesterday, the heir to Fujiwara Medicines Inc, Kenji Fujiwara, was rushed into hospital after he died choking on his dinner.

The only people in the room were his wife,Kiyiko Fujiwara and their 5 year old son, Riku. According to staff, it was the son who hit the panic button to call for help.

Forensic tests have revealed that the cause of death was cyanide poisoning. The source of the poison was the spicy soup that he had ingested.
