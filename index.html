Psychic Detective

Hello world

Welcome to the world of psychic detectives! The year is 2030, and unbeknownst to the rest of the world, Japan has genetically engineered a race of memory Readers.

As one of those memory readers, you are tasked with solving a series of high profile crimes. You do this by choosing a series of the questions, and at the end. judging if the defendant is innocent or guilty. Remember, because of your psychic abilities your judgement will bypass the judical system, and the guilty party will be sent to prison on your word.
